Heads of State (2025)

Overview

When Air Force One is taken down over hostile territory, U.S. President Will Derringer and British Prime Minister Sam Clarke — bitter rivals on the world stage — are forced to team up. On the run with no backup and surrounded by enemies, they must survive the odds and unravel a high-stakes conspiracy that threatens global security. Politics, power, and pride collide in this explosive action-thriller.
